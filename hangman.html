Pokengman - Guess the right word!

Oh no! Jigglypuff used sing!

Jigglypuff wanted to serenade her best friend Pikachu. However, her song is made Pikachu very drowsy!

How to Play:

  • Guess the correct word to wake Pikachu up!
  • Guess the word one letter at a time
  • If you guess the wrong letter, Jigglypuff will get upset and draw on Pikachu
  • You have 6 tries before Jigglypuff bursts into tears!
